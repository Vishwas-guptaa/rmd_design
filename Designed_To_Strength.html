At Radha Madhav Dham, every type of devotional programs are a unique spiritual experience designed for different audiences but with one goal in mind - to teach the practical aspects of Hindu spirituality and provide an opportunity to make devotional progress while connecting with like minded families.

Akhand Sankirtan

• Get closer to the goal of God realization

• 18 hours of chanting daily: 6am – 12pm

• 5 days of non-stop sankirtan

Sadhana Shivir

• Designed for devotional progress

• 2 weeks every Summer (June/July)

• Includes Rath Yatra Celebration

• No classes for youth, only music lessons

Family Camp

• Held annually from Dec 25-31

• Parallel classes for youth & adults by age

• Learn Hinduism, Music, Do Seva & Sadhana
